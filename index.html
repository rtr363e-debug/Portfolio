About Me

My School Life

Life at school has its fluctuations. Grand tests often show up on Fridays — tests teachers insist on — and the pressure can be real. But with great friends who chat, joke, and support me, school becomes a place of memories and small victories. I laugh, learn, and find music and shows that keep my mind alive even during the busiest days.
